Cry Another Day

Google Trend Analysis by Raghu, Satheesh & Soumya

Women's Day

In The year 2013 Women's day made it to top google trends continuously for 20 plus days from 9th to 31st of March . Women's day actually falls on 8th March, but unfortunately, it did not Trend on that day. Looks like a lot of men in 2013 were bashed heavily for not remembering it and they continuously googled about it for next 20 days.

Women's Day is highly correlated with Mother's day. Should I explain why? Haven't you seen enough movies which compares every women with mother?

Valentine's Day

Unlike Women's day Valentine's day managed to make it to top trends every year on valentines day. In the years 2010 and 2012 Valentine's day started trending as early as 3rd of Feb. In the year 2014, Valentine's day first made it to the trend on 8th of Feb but it went on to stay in the trend until 24th Feb. Go easy guys...

Valentine's Day is highly correlated with teacher's day ! The Malar effect? May be...

Friendship Day

Friendship day does not have a particular date. It is celebrated on the first Sunday of August. Surprisingly, this year 2016 Friendship day made it to the trends on Feb 5th. Maybe, people these days are so very confused between Love and Friendship.

Friendship Day is highly correlated with teachers day. Yes, ofcourse teachers are very friendly before you join and after passout from their school.

Independence Day

3 times independence day has made it to the google trends in the month of January and few times republic day has managed to make to the trends in the month of August. Looks like there are lot of Indians who are yet to make out of fifth standard civics class hangover.

Independence day is highly correlated with Labours day. After all we are all labours of freedom and independence.

Engineers Day

Engineers Day falls on 15th September every year. It has made to google trends for 5 years and in all these 5 years not even once it has made an appearance before 15th of September. Engineers, on time every time.

Engineer's day is highly correlated with Father's day. Yes, when you celebrate engineer's day you should also celebrate the day of the person who forced you to pursue it.
