FPL Fixture Analysis

About the App

Everyone follows football will surely know about this game Fantasy Premier League. You fight with your friends throughout the season for nothing but just bragging rights of having a better tactical footballing mind.
This app is an attempt to improve the decision making of choosing players based on the upcoming fixtures in the near future through visual analytics. This will be updated week in week out for all the game weeks. I will also make sure to constantly improve the UX, UI and also try including additional features as the game progresses.
I am open to all your suggestions for improvements, we can connect through the SM handles mentioned below. Happy Playing.
