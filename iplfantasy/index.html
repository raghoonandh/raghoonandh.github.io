Hello World,
I have been playing this IPL fantasy for quite some years now. Since the fixture distribution among the teams is not symmetric, every time it is pain to go through fixtures and carefully make transfers so that I don't mistakenly transfer out a player who plays next day and retain a player from a team which has gone for an industrial visit to Goa. I have had enough, and for this year I have written a program to do it for me. As I had some free time, I build this app to help you pick teams based on the schedule, home game advantage with little math and logic. There are two modes Crazy and Lazy. Crazy is for people who do transfers very frequently and Lazy is for people do transfers team once in a while. This app auto refreshes every day with updated order. So come back every time before you do a transfer. Happy Fantasying.
