The best part about life is that it is a circle. In life, you always end up doing things close to what you were actually aspiring to do. While I was doing my engineering I wanted to become a copywriter and come up with crazy quotes for brands. But there were a series of events in life that happened in my life and I am data specialist now, a person who codes basically. I Learnt that there is not much of difference between copywriter and code writer except for the fact that you need to know few languages apart from English like java, HTML, CSS etc. and your writing should be sensible at least for the computer to understand.
I have been posting a lot of quotes, status messages, one-liners for several years now. I was able to fetch all of it few days ago thanks to the api. I Was surprised to know I have posted almost close to 1000 such things belonging to several. Have put across 1/3rd of it (333) in using few languages I have learnt.
