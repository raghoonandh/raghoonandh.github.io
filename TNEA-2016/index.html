TNEA HEAT MAP 2016

About the app

It is that time of the year, when the heat wave is taking a grip on the state and any talk about the cut off marks and counselling will bring up a heated argument in any household. This heatmap is for our protogonist, who is in the midst of all this mess with a counselling card which has a date and few numbers around it. This app is an attempt to replace the traditional way of entering your cut off marks in a website, which gives you a table with few more numbers. We are not MS excel or SQL to consume tables. After all, we are humans and we deserve better. Don't we?

How it Works

Max of 100 seats from various departments & colleges which had closing cut off mark around that of yours from last year (2015) is shown. Eg If your cut off is 193, seats with closing cut offs varying from 195 to 193 would be shown. The color represents the probabality of getting it. Darker the blue higher the chances of getting it. Lighter the green slimmer the chances. Hover over the square to find the course-college. Click on it to find more details, and wait for a song to be played in the background based on your probability of getting that seat (Works only on Desktop Browser)
